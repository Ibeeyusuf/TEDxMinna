About TEDxMinna Partnership

Licensed by TED, TEDx events provide local communities with an opportunity to bring people together to discuss ideas that change our world and share a TED-like experience. Our parent organization, TED, reaches millions of people a day and connects with billions through its online presence.

TEDxMinna is a local and global platform for IDEAS WORTH SPREADING. Over the last five years, we have reached over 300,000 people at our live events and more than a million through our videos.

TEDxMinna is offering a unique opportunity for brands to be on the international TEDx platform as well as its independently organized local connection. In line with the global brand of TED, TEDxMinna is aimed at amplifying ideas worth sharing through unheard voices. While Ideas build the nation, funds are the ingredient that nourishes ideas. We are in search of partners who share our vision and want to be a part of this mind-shaping TED event and foster the spread of innovative ideas.

This Year's Theme: "Beyond the Noise"

In a world saturated with sound, opinions, and distractions, what does it mean to listen, think deeply, and act intentionally?

This year's theme, "Beyond the Noise", explores how we rise above the chatter to discover purpose, insight, and action. Through this theme, we invite our audience to move beyond the noise—to reflect, reconnect, and reimagine.

Our Focus Areas:

  • Listening to overlooked voices
  • Finding clarity in confusion
  • Cutting through information overload
  • Reclaiming stillness, focus, and truth

2020

Changing Mindsets: Our First TEDxMinna

2020 what a year. If you asked anyone what they remembered about 2020, they would scream Corona Virus Pandemic and how it shaped their lives. It also shaped ours.

Our first TEDxMinna was slated to be held on 21st March, 2020 at Hon. Justice Idris Legbo Kutigi International conference centre, but Corona happened and it was rescheduled. It was held on 12th September 2020, and as you already know, it was a success.

The theme was changing Mindsets- A shift in how people think is vital to community development. There is always room to unlearn and relearn; the mind should never be limited to a belief but rather be open to embracing new ideas and approaches.

Before the TEDx event day, the Curator Suleiman Isah, who is currently the Commissioner of Technology of Niger State, held a series of live discussions with the speakers, which were aligned with their profession in regards to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Environmental Impact: 30 trees planted at the Bridget Aisha Lemu park
2021

One Voice: TEDxMinna 2021

Minna is a melting pot of culture, creativity, and resilience. "One Voice" unites artists, intellectuals, activists, and dreamers who share a goal to create a better future. It acknowledges differences rather than erases them.

One Voice was selected as the theme because of the need for the unity of the community.

How can a nation grow when the people are not divided? When people are divided by tribe, religion, class, gender, or generation — progress slows. But when we come together with one voice, even if it is made up of many tones, we become louder, stronger, and impossible to ignore.

Unity doesn't mean we all agree on everything — it means we align around shared values, like justice, dignity, and hope.

Event Details:
Date: 25th September 2021
Venue: Hon. Justice Idris Legbo Kutigi International Conference Centre
